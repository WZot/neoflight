Pilot Briefing

NavigationUse Joysticks or [W,A,S,D] + [ARROWS] to fly. Hold your inputs to keep throttle.
LandingTouch down on ground or roofs to enter a safe “Landed” state (battery no longer depletes). While landed, lateral movement is disabled until you command takeoff.
RTH ProtocolEngages autonomous return to home for landing. Manual input can override it.
SCAN / RADAR PINGPress SCAN (or Q) to sweep the minimap and highlight targets, crates, powerups, and threats.
WINDEnable WIND for gust drift during storms (or dynamic weather).
HEATWhen enabled, aggressive flight builds heat. Land to cool down. Overheat disables turbo.
HACK (Mini-Game)When enabled, fly near a red intel node. Watch the moving timing window: progress speeds up when the marker sits inside the green safe zone. Click SYNC to boost when you time it.
THREATSPatrol drones periodically lock on. Look for red threat dots on the minimap and the THREAT CONTACT HUD indicator. While locked, signal drops faster and battery drains faster.
MODULESAfter a mission, spend points to improve heat sinks, hack assist, and radar suite effects.
